Get more people on bikes. Build a crew that shows up. Create stories worth telling. Look cool in orange.

the vibe

Chill long rides. We go for the journey, bring tunes along, but always make sure it's an adventure.

07

frequently asked questions

yes, you can wear lycra. no, you don't have to.

do i need to be fast?

Nope. You just need to be willing. We ride at all levels and no one gets left behind. We're in it for the journey, not the destination.

what kind of bike do i need?

Road, gravel, fixie, your nan's hybrid... if it has two wheels, you're good. We're about the ride, not the price tag.

when and where do you ride?

Usually weekend long rides, but occasionally a nice out and back on the weekdays. Times and routes drop in the group chat.

do i have to wear orange?

It's strongly encouraged. We're building a brand here. Plus you'll look incredible. BBBC kit drops coming soon.

is there a membership fee?

Free. Always. This isn't some posh cycling club. Just bring yourself, your bike, and maybe buy a round of coffees once in a while.

is it only for men?

Absolutely not. BBBC is fully gender neutral — everyone is welcome. We're also very open to renaming it Bad Girls Bike Club or even Baddies Bike Club. Honestly, Baddies Bike Club goes pretty hard.
